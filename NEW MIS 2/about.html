Our Mission

To empower students with a rigorous interdisciplinary education that fuses advanced quantitative analytics with deep psychological market understanding. We aim to produce leaders who transform complex datasets into ethical, actionable business strategies.

DATA SCIENCE MARKETING ETHICS

Our Vision

To be the premier destination for consumer analytics in the Midwest, recognized globally for innovation in understanding the human element within digital ecosystems.

Our Story

Founded at the intersection of UMD’s Labovitz School of Business and Economics and the growing demand for data-literate marketing professionals, the Consumer Insight & Analytics major was born from a simple observation: businesses had plenty of data, but very little understanding.

Since our inception, we have grown from a niche concentration into a flagship major. We’ve built partnerships with Fortune 500 companies and local startups alike, creating a living laboratory where students apply classroom theory to real-world market challenges.

Today, our alumni lead departments at major global brands, proving that the blend of Duluth's grit and analytical precision is a powerful catalyst for professional success.
